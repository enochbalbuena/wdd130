Puebla

Puebla is a state in the south-center of Mexico. It is bordered by the states of Veracruz to the north and east, Hidalgo, México, Tlaxcala, and Morelos to the west, and Guerrero and Oaxaca to the south. The state is a center of colonial architecture and culinary innovation, and a major tourist destination for its historic buildings and vibrant cultural festivals. The state capital and largest city is Puebla City, which is renowned for its stunning architecture and rich history.
