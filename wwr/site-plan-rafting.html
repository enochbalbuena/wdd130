Overview

Purpose

This website will introduce our Rafting Site as a top choice for water rafting. It will talk about how we make sure our trips are safe, fun, and good for everyone. We'll show the different kinds of rafting trips we offer, from easy ones for beginners to more challenging ones for people who have done rafting before.

Audience

The audience are families looking for a special trip, and companies that want to do team activities. We want to attract people who enjoy being outdoors and trying new things.
